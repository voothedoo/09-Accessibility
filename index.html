Romania

Mici

Mici, Romania's beloved street food, are skinless sausages made from a mixture of minced meat, garlic, and spices. Grilled to perfection, they boast a juicy, flavorful taste. Served with mustard and fresh bread, mici are a culinary delight and a testament to Romanian culinary ingenuity.

Where History Meets Natural Beauty

Traveling in Romania offers a unique blend of history, natural beauty, and vibrant culture. Explore medieval castles, picturesque villages, and the stunning Carpathian Mountains. Taste traditional cuisine, such as sarmale and mămăligă, and witness the warm hospitality of the locals. Romania is a captivating destination for every traveler.

Slovakia

Bryndzové halušky

Halusky, a traditional Slovak dish, consists of soft potato dumplings served with various toppings, often including bryndza cheese and crispy bacon. This hearty comfort food is a beloved part of Slovak cuisine, offering a rich and satisfying taste of the country's culinary heritage.

Unveiling a Traveler's Paradise.

Traveling in Slovakia reveals a hidden gem in Central Europe. Explore the charming capital, Bratislava, and the historic towns of Banska Stiavnica and Kosice. Immerse yourself in the Tatra Mountains' natural beauty and experience authentic Slovak cuisine. Slovakia offers a unique blend of history, culture, and stunning landscapes.

Azores Islands

Cozido

Cozido is a traditional dish from the Azores, Portugal. This flavorful stew is made by slow-cooking a mix of meats, vegetables, and spices in the natural heat of volcanic springs. It's a true culinary gem that captures the essence of this stunning archipelago.

A Natural Wonder in the Atlantic.

Traveling in the Azores is a journey into a pristine paradise. Explore volcanic landscapes, lush greenery, and azure waters. Witness geothermal springs and lush hiking trails on these Portuguese islands. The Azores offer a unique, unspoiled beauty that's perfect for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.
